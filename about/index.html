About Plan and Thrive

Plan and Thrive is a business consulting and coaching firm dedicated to helping individuals and companies grow, rendering real results and true fulfillment.

One of our main purposes is to promote the idea that organizations and businesses are not only meant to generate money, but also to generate results as they make stakeholders richer in a wholesome way.

This contributes to a more self-fulfilling life for their people and therefore for the community.

Ana Orozco is the founding partner of Plan and Thrive. She delivers consulting services to growing small and middle sized companies, based on her groundbreaking RE Plan Model.

Meet Ana Orozco

Ana Sofia Orozco is a professional coach, business consultant and public speaker.

For more than two decades she has been dedicated to the exploration and understanding of how the interactions between the rational and emotional sides of ourselves, determine the success in which we individuals, or our organizations, accomplish goals and THRIVE.

She obtained a degree in the Arts in 1996 and a second degree in Business Administration in 1997. She has an international MBA and an Advanced Certification in Executive Coaching and Organizational Behavior.

She worked as a consultant for about 10 years, helping companies worldwide in different areas: finances, strategic planning, organizational behavior, and human development. She has worked with companies such as Telefonica, Repsol, Avis, Endesa, BBVA, and many others in different countries.

She started her own consulting company in 2006 delivering entrepreneurial consulting and mentoring services to sole proprietor businesses and small companies.

She became a professional coach in 2008 and has specialized in the areas of self-awareness, self-leadership, success, change management, life balance and wellness coaching.

She currently also works as a business consultant and advisor for small and medium sized companies, helping them grow in a conscientious and structured manner, developing their corporate effectiveness and their human talent.

Ana continuously teaches workshops internationally, focusing mainly in the areas of Success, Change, Self-Leadership, Life Balance and General Wellness.
